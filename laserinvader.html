Game Overview

Laser Invader is a 2D space shooter themed game. In this game, you control a spaceship and your objective is to survive the waves of enemy spaceships by shooting them down and dodging their bullets.

Contributions

  • Core Functionality: I designed and developed the core gameplay functionalities, including player movement, shooting mechanics, and collision detection.
  • Health Systems: I implemented the health point systems for both the player and enemy units, ensuring a balanced and challenging gaming experience.
  • Singleton Patterns: To enhance efficiency and organization, I utilized the Singleton pattern for crucial components, such as the Audio Player, Scorekeeper, and Level Manager.
  • Scriptable Objects: I leveraged scriptable objects to create dynamic waypoints and paths for the enemy waves, adding depth to the gameplay.
  • Menu System: I developed an efficient and user-friendly menu system, enhancing the game's accessibility and overall polish.
  • Level Design: I took charge of crafting the overall level design, ensuring a gradual increase in difficulty and a compelling player journey.
  • Playtesting and Balancing: I actively participated in playtesting and fine-tuned various game parameters to achieve optimal gameplay balance.
