Project information

  • Genre:Open World,3D,FPS Shooter,Survival,Zombie
  • Player Experience:Intense
  • Project date:November 17,2022
  • Tools Used:Unity(C#)
  • Source Code: Github
  • Play here: itch.io

Main Objective

Stranded in an inhabited island, with a load of guns and a dying flashlight,you find you are not alone. Escape your way out of this zombie infested island!

What I did

  • Designed and developed the core player functionalities such as movement,shooting and health.
  • Contructed enemy AI for following players using NavMeshAI along with animations for different states.
  • Developed a unique dying flashlight mechanism to increase difficulty.
  • Contructed a health point system for enemy as well as player.
  • Designed and developed efficient ammo system for different ammo types for different weapons.
  • Designed and developed several pickups such as ammo and battery/health powerups.
  • Implemented the scriptable objects functionalities for different interactables such as dialogues for better experience.
  • Developed UI system for ammo,health and waypoint system.
  • Developed a simple level manager for handling different menus.
  • Contructed a ambience mechanism for better player experience.
  • Contructed an overall level design of the game.
  • Worked on playtesting and overall balancing of the game.
